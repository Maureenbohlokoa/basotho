Traditional Clothing

Uncover the diversity of Basotho clothes. From their blankets with divine patterns that tell acient Basotho stories to their straped Thethanas and many more.

TRADITIONAL CLOTHES

Basotho traditional clothes are really colorful and fun. Girls wear thithana, which are bright skirts and pretty tops. Everyone likes seshweshwe because it has cool patterns. Boys wear tsheea, which are special pants, and shirts made from seshweshwe. The clothes are happy and show Basotho culture

TRADIONAL BLANKETS AND HATS

Basotho have really cool hats and blankets. The hats are called mokorotlo and they look like tall cones. People wear them on their heads to show they are Basotho. The blankets are big and warm and have nice patterns. Everyone wears them to stay cozy and look proud of their culture.

VARIOUS WAYS TO STYLE TRADITIONAL SHWESHWE AND BLANKETS

Basotho seshweshwe clothes and blankets are really colorful and have cool patterns. People can make them into many different clothes, like dresses, skirts, shirts, and jackets. Even when they are made in new styles, they still show Basotho culture and look really special. You can wear them for parties, school, or just to be proud of being Basotho.
